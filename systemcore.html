The 2027
control system.

Starting in 2027, the roboRIO and the radio retire and a single new board, SystemCore, takes over. This short course gathers what is known so far: the hardware, the new software, and how to get your team ready.

⚠ Alpha preview · this will change

SystemCore is still in alpha and beta testing as of 2026. Specs, wiring, software, and even some of the facts below are not final and will change before the 2027 kickoff. Treat this course as a living preview, not a build guide, and always confirm details against the official sources linked at the bottom.

We built it anyway so your team can start learning the shape of the change early. We will update it as the real thing firms up.
